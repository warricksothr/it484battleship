Manual Scripts

Test One: Changing Style
  1. Click 'Options'
  2. Click 'Change Style'
  3. Verify style change
Test Two: Main Menu
  1. Verify 'Play Game', 'Options' buttons are present
  2. Click 'Options'
  3. Verify 'Change Theme', 'Turn Sound On/Off, and 'Return to Home' options are present.
  4. Click 'Return to Home'
  5. Click 'Play Game'
  6. Verify user is given 'One Player', 'Two Player', 'Crazy One Player', 'Crazy Two Player', and 'Return to Home' options
Test Three: Game Interface
  1. Click 'Play Game'
  2. Click 'Two Player Game'
  3. Verify players are given 1 visible grid (centered) with a 'Ship Views' button in lower right corner
  4. Hover over 'Grid Views' button
  5. Verify grid view changes to show ships
  6. Verify each grid is 10x10 squares
  7. Verify columns are identified with numbers(1-10)
  8. Verify rows are identified with letters (A-J)
  9. Verify each player is given exactly 5 ships of sizes (5, 4, 3, 3, 2)
  10. Verify 'Take Your Shot...' at top of screen changes correctly for each player
Test Four: Ship Placement
  1. Click 'Play Game'
  2. Click 'Two Player Game'
  3. Verify user has the ability to move ship onto, and around grid ship grid
  4. Verify ships can only be placed vertically or horizontally
  5. Verify user cannot place ship off of grid
  6. Verify user cannot stack ships on top of one another
  7. Verify user must place exactly 5 ships on board (no more, no less)
Test Five: Shot Taking
  1. Click 'Play Game'
  2. Click 'Two Player Game'
  3. Set up initial ship positions for each player
  4. Verify ships are displayed on the ship grid
  5. Verify user takes a shot by clicking a grid cell on their shot grid
  6. Verify a graphical display is shown to indicate a shot was taken at that spot (colored box), and differentiate if it was a hit or miss (gray box vs. red box)
  7. Take a shot at the same square more than once
  8. Verify that this doesn't cause any functionality issues. This should be allowed, and not cause ship to take a new damaging hit
  9. Verify that shots are being recorded correctly in 'History' section on left side of screen
Test Six: Ship Sinking
  1. Click 'Play Game'
  2. Click 'Two Player Game'
  3. Set up initial ship positions for each player
  4. Continue clicking on a ship's squares until the ship is sunk
  5. Verify that a prompt is displayed alerting the player that they have sunk a ship. Also make sure the ship name is correct for the sunk ship
  6. Repeat this for all ships
Test Seven: Human vs. Computer
  1. Click 'Play Game'
  2. Click 'One Player Game'
  3. Verify player is required to place ships (in accordance with placement test)
  4. Verify computer ships are placed automatically (User should not see any prompts)
  5. Verify that player gets the first shot
  6. Take a shot as the player
  7. Hover over 'Ship Views' during next turn
  8. Verify that a gray box is displayed, indicating that the computer has taken a shot
  9. Continue taking several shots. Verify that the computer is taking a shot after every player shot
  10. Verify game ends appropriately when either user ships are all sunk, or computer ships are all sunk (whichever comes first)
Test Eight: Special Abilities
  1. Click 'Play Game'
  2. Click 'Crazy One Player'
  3. Place ships on board and start game
  4. Click each special shot without firing
  5. Verify the highlighted selection changes correctly
  6. Click and fire 'Photon Bomb Run'
  7. Verify length of shot is 3 squares and that cooldown timer is says 7
  8. Click and fire 'Black Hole'
  9. Verify a 5-shot plus shaped area is shot and that cooldown timer says 11
  10. Verify photon bomb run cooldown decreases by 1
  11. Click and fire 'Scrap Radar'
  12. Verify a 5-shot plus shaped area is highlighted and that cooldown timer says 5
  13. Verify previous shot cooldowns all decrease by 1
  14. Click and fire 'Plasma Salvo'
  15. Verify 3 random squares on the grid are shot and that cooldown timer says 3
  16. Verify previous shot cooldowns all decrease by 1
  17. Click and fire 'Homing Missile'
  18. Verify a ship is hit and that cooldown timer says 5
  19. Verify previous shot cooldowns all decrease by 1
  20. Continue firing regular shot until cooldowns expire
  21. Verify that use of the shots is gained again when cooldown expires
Test Nine: Ship Sunk = Shot Gone
  1. Click 'Play Game'
  2. Click 'Crazy One Player'
  3. Place ships on board and start game
  4. Only take regular shots, make sure not to win the game
  5. As game progresses, verify that when a ship is sunk, its ability is removed from the special shots menu. Continue until all of your ships are sunk. The ships/shots are as follows:
    • Stardust Goliath (length 5) - Photon Bomb Run
    • Galaxy Ravager (length 4) - Black Hole
    • Scrap Harvester (length 3) - Scrap Radar
    • Star Destroyer (length 3) - Plasma Salvo
    • Interceptor (length 2) - Homing Missile
